    Legend & glossary

    Reading the Progress column

    Each bill is placed on a 5-step track: Introduced → Committee → 1st reading → Final reading → Enacted.

    • Step reached / completed
    • Current step (still active)
    • Stalled — postponed, deferred, or held
    • Failed — defeated or withdrawn
    • Status not yet reported

    The date below each track is the most recent action on that bill.
